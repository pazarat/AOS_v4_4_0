# Execution Gates and No Guessing Canon

Execution is not allowed when required owner truth is missing, conflicting, or incomplete.

## Required before writing or implementation

- intent and surface resolution,
- project or runtime scope lock,
- truth anchor,
- applicable workshop lens,
- owner discovery,
- duplicate and conflict scan,
- impact scan,
- missing-dimension classification,
- patch or delivery plan,
- approval gate for high-risk changes,
- validation and receipt.

## Forbidden

- executing from a folder name alone,
- treating a generic best practice as local truth,
- writing a parallel concept without owner search,
- claiming absence without a scoped zero-result scan,
- upgrading a candidate into a decision without evidence,
- treating documented as execution-ready without maturity coverage.
